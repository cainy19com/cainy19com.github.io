$n& is number of trials,
each trail has two outcomes,
and is independent

$x$ is number of success,
$n-x$ is number of failure

$p$ is probability of success,
$1-p$ is prob of failure,
$p$ is constant for all trails

---

```meth
\displaystyle
\binom n x =
\frac{n!}{x!(n-x)!}

\\[10px]

\displaystyle
\binom 5 3 =
\frac{5!}{3!\cdot (5-3)!} =
10
```

---

Formula

```meth
\displaystyle
\binom n x
p^x
(1-p)^{n-x}
```
